About The Founder..

Namaste! I'm Swathi, a passionate YouTuber and proud mother of my miracle, Minnie. Starting Minnie Inti Ruchulu was a very personal journey for me. After getting married, my life became a busy mix of working, managing the house, and taking care of my child. Finding time to eat a proper meal was hard. During those hectic days, I often thought about the delicious dishes my mother used to make and how much I missed spending time with my daughter.One day, I thought—why not share my mom's wonderful recipes with everyone? This way, others can also enjoy tasty, homemade meals. I decided to take a break from my job and put all my energy into creating Minnie Inti Ruchulu. In a short time, Minnie Inti Ruchulu became very successful. This encouraged me to focus completely on this venture. Now, surrounded by my kids, I am happy to send love through healthy and tasty food to everyone.Join us at Minnie Inti Ruchulu, where each product is made with love and a touch of tradition, ensuring you enjoy the warmth of homemade goodness in every bite.
